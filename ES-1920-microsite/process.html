Process

Working on 'Psych' had many challenges and breakthrough moments. Here are the steps I took for my project.

Psych.

Psych. is an audio-visual project using the MakeNoise (audio) and P5JS (visuals). This project has allowed me to be creative through the means of learning to work with codes for a project.

Initial Idea

My first sketch was something I still find beautiful. I've always loved simplicity. My initial idea was to keep the project monochrome, with subtle movements.

Second Sketch

I decided to not go with monochrome as theme for the video after realising how certain colours and movements compliment each other.

Interest

After researching about visuals, I fell in the love with the works done by Joanie LeMercier as it was simple yet so captivating to the eyes. His works were up my alley as it was repetitive yet clean and sharp.

Visuals and Code Direction

As for my visuals, I kept it simple by using shapes and lines. Also, WEBGL and loops were a constant in almost all my codes. I've also decided to vary the same sketches in different forms and colours.

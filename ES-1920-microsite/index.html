Project Description

Psych. is an audio-visual project using the MakeNoise (audio) and P5JS (visuals). For this project, I have circled my ideas around shapes and lines.

As this is my first time working with both visuals and audio, I've decided to keep things simple and to a minimum. I guess the title 'Psych.' sits well with the soundtrack as the sounds used were heavily procressed and driven. As much as we all are captivated and intrigued by things we don’t see very often, I chose to create visuals that are safe. These are shapes we tend to see on a daily basis. They are geometric shapes; and these shapes takes the form of whatever we see on a daily basis. My inspiration was from Joanie Lemercier’s works.
